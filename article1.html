A Life-Long Story

It was 1939. Anna saw a platoon of soldiers and noticed a young face with sky-colored eyes. It was love at first sight. Since that evening, they had met every day. A few months later, Mikhail went to the front. She could not write him because the frontline lost connection. Meanwhile, her belly was getting bigger... After 20 years in the city square, she saw painfully familiar features, and her heart began to beat faster. His loving blue gaze was directed at her because true love knows no time or distance. It is always with you, no matter what happens.
